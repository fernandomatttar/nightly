Frequently asked questions

Why does Windows show a security warning during installation?

That blue "Windows protected your PC" screen is Windows SmartScreen. It appears for new independent software that hasn't built up a download reputation yet — it is not a virus warning. Click "More info", then "Run anyway" to continue. See our Security page for the full explanation.

Is Nightly safe?

Yes. Nightly only automates the Night Light control already built into Windows. It collects no data, connects to no servers, and runs no background process.

Does it work on Windows 10?

No. Nightly relies on the Windows 11 Night Light interface (build 25H2 / 26200 or newer).

How do I change the strength after installing?

With Nightly Pro, open "Configure Nightly" from the Start menu, pick a preset or enter a custom value, and save. No reinstall needed. Nightly Free uses a fixed 65%.

What do I get when I buy Pro?

A download of Nightly-Pro-v1.0.0.zip containing the Pro installer plus the Installation Guide, Security Guide, License, and Changelog — delivered right after checkout.

Can I upgrade from Free to Pro later?

Absolutely. Install Pro over your Free copy any time — your existing settings are kept.
