Privacy policy

Last updated: 2026

The Nightly app

The Nightly application collects no personal data. It does not create an account, does not connect to the internet, and does not include any analytics or tracking. It only switches the Night Light setting that is already part of Windows. Your chosen strength is stored in a small text file on your own computer and never leaves it.

This website

This website uses Cloudflare Web Analytics to understand how many people visit and which pages are useful. It is privacy-first: it uses no cookies, does not fingerprint you, and does not track you across other websites. It does not collect personally identifiable information.

Purchases

Nightly Pro is sold through a third-party checkout provider. When you buy, that provider processes your payment and email address under its own privacy policy in order to deliver your download and receipt. We never see or store your full payment details.
